Kadai Paneer

Ingredients

  1. paeer
  2. Onion
  3. Tomato Paste
  4. Capsicum
  5. Green Chilli
  6. Ginger Garlic Paste
  7. kasuri Methi
  8. All Masalas
  9. oil n Salt

How to Cook

  1. Heat a pan with half a tablespoon oil on a high flame.
  2. Saute 1 heaped cup of cubed onions along with 6 to 7 split cashews until they turn transparent or lightly golden.
  3. Add 1 cup chopped tomatoes and continue to saute on medium heat until tomatoes turn mushy and soft.
  4. Let it cool down for a few minutes
  5. Then add onion mixture to a blender jar
  6. Blend it to smooth paste without adding water.
  7. Add onion tomato puree.
  8. To the gravy, add 3/4 cup water and mix well. Bring this to a boil. If needed pour another 1/4 cup water.
  9. Mix well. Add chopped coriander leaves. Cover and set aside. Do not leave the stove on since it will continue to cook further. Transfer to a serving bowl.
